About Me

I was born the third of two children -- "Surprise! Hope is not a strategy." My birth name is not really Fluffy; I conceded to mother's dimentia for the treats. Art school was just a ruse to get my parents to pay for....art school! Being a starving artist in my 20's was "trendy" but, once I hit my 30's and my metabolism stopped working, trendy was replaced with narcissism. Comedy once saved my life, so I had to kill it.
